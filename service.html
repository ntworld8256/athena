Our Service

Athena Education Resources Limited is a registered company in Nigeria. Our aim is to assist interested Nigerian to access international education and training both for students and working class. In the last four years, over 1500 Nigerian students has travelled outside Nigeria for higher education in various parts of the world and through our training services, we have run programme to government worked inform of capacity building in Nigeria and UK. Athena is a gateway into study and training programme in India, China, Malaysia, Sweden, Czech Republic and Germany. Also through our capacity building programme, government workers of all shades and other cooperate executive can be trained and improve their ability through our UK training programme. If you are looking for qualitative services in international admission/placement then you have come to a right place.

FOREIGN ADMISSIONS PROCESSING

We process foreign admission for interested Nigerians who are willing to study abroad from first degree to advanced degree level, in our partner institutions or any universities of their choices around the world.

FOREIGN SHORT DURATION MANAGEMENT AND OCCUPATIONAL TRAINING PROGRAMESG

Among many other services we provides, is capacity building programs for governments workers in core ministries, perastatals, government agencies and instrumentalities. Training for cooperate executive in private sectors, is also available.

LOCAL OCCUPATIONAL TRAINING PROGRAMME

We provide local occupational training programs for those who are in education sectors and oil and gas respectively. We trained educational administrators in various capacity building. However, those in oil and gas can also benefit from our training programs in piping design courses delivered by our training partners in UK, Canada, India, China etc.

DISTANCE LEARNING PROGRAMME FOR OVERSEA INSTITUTION

Part of our services is organizing distance learning programs for individuals who are interested in top-up and continuous education programs through our oversea recognized universities and institutions.

REPRESENTATION OF INTERNATIONAL UNIVERSITIES AND INSTITUTION
