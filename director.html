Director's Speech

MR. ADENIYI MOSHOOD A.

CEO/MD, Athena Education Resources Limited.

He is a native of Ilogbo Ekiti in Ido/Osi Local Government Area of Ekiti State, Nigeria. Mr. Adebayo holds Associate Accounting Technician Certificate (AAT) 1997 of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN), Intermediate Professional Examination of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (2000).
Mr. Adeniyi Moshood A. started his career in 1992 at Ondo State Polytechnic, Owo (Now Rufus Giwa Polytechnic), Ondo State. He worked in various departments such as Admissions Office, Establishments Office, Physical Planning Unit and Bursary.

He joined the service of National Business and Technical Examinations Board (NABTEB), National Headquarters Benin City as Finance/Budget Assistant in 1998. He became the pioneer Zonal Accountant of Awka Zonal Office of National Business and Technical Examinations Board, (NABTEB) Awka Anambra state in 2003.
In 2006, he was employed as Assistant Finance/Admin Coordinator of Nigerian Institute of Industrial Management (NIIM) where he resigned to start private business in 2009. In 2011, he was sponsored by Government of India under ITEC programme for three months programme in Human Resource and Entrepreneurship Development/Education Trading at National Institute for Entrepreneurship and Small Business Development (NIESBUD), Noida, India. He was adjudged the most serious student and he was the group leader of 39 delegates from 19 countries.
Michael Adebayo has attended many seminars on educational development in Nigeria. He has also written two books which are: Students Guide to Examination Success; and The Making of A Good Student Michael Adebayo is married with a son, he loves writing and reading as hobbies
